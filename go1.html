ROBB

  • Name: Robb
  • Age: 17
  • Gender: Male
  • Likes: Winter, Meat
  • Dislikes: Persistency, The weak
  • Family: Rob(twin brother)

ABOUT

Robb is a main character from my old visual novel project "HIGH SCHOOL" (i'm not very creative with names).

He is very bright but does not get along very well with other people because of his arrogance.

HISTORY

Robb comes from an average family unlike the other students in FAEH HIGH. He has a twin brother named Rob and they were inseparable since birth. Because of their names being almost identical(they're identical twins too), people just read his name with prolonged B's (Like Robbbbbbb-). In his grade school days, he never did have any friends and he only had his brother with him. Everything changed in High School. There, he met the main protagonist which I didn't name yet. Their personalities clash but in the end, they became good friends.

(Yes, since this is an OTOME game, he is a dateable character. *wink wink*)

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
